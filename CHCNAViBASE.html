Surveying and Engineering

Highlights

The iBase GNSS receiver offers a streamlined solution as a GNSS base station designed to meet surveyor’s needs. Its performance rivals that of a standard external UHF radio modem without the bulk of external batteries, cables, radios, and antennas. With a 5-watt radio module, it provides GNSS RTK coverage up to 25 km under optimal conditions. Its real-time UHF interference self-checking capability allows operators to select the best frequency channel, increasing ease of use and efficiency. In addition, iBase's integrated AUTO-IMU enhances its functionality as a GNSS rover.

Benefits and Performances

Product Features and Highlights

Integrated and Rugged Design

Eliminate the need for external batteries and cables with the iBase’s integrated design. Its industrial-grade build meets IP67 standards for dust and water resistance and has an IK08 shock rating for durability.

Comprehensive GNSS Tracking

Ensure superior accuracy with the iBase's 1408-channel GNSS technology. It supports GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, BeiDou, QZSS and SBAS constellations and PPP service, providing dependable RTK corrections and improving survey accuracy and efficiency.

Advanced Multipath Mitigation

Improve positioning accuracy with advanced multipath mitigation algorithms. The iBase ensures that high-quality GNSS corrections are transmitted to rovers for optimal performance in challenging environments.

Long-Range UHF Transmission

Achieve wide field coverage with the iBase’s built-in 5-watt UHF radio module. It provides GNSS RTK coverage up to 25 km under optimal conditions, ensuring uninterrupted data transmission over long distances.

Real-Time UHF Interference Checking

Improve efficiency with the iBase’s real-time UHF interference self-checking capability allowing operators to select the most appropriate frequency channel for optimal performance.

Extended Battery Life

Perform extended field operations with the iBase’s dual hot-swappable high-capacity batteries. The advanced electronic design reduces power consumption, ensuring efficient use and extended operating time.
