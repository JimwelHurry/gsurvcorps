Highlights

The CTS-A100 is a reflectorless total station optimized for efficient fieldwork, featuring the Android OS and CHCNAV's LandStar software for intuitive, high-performance surveying. Its powerful EDM system offers a range of 5000 m with a prism and 1500 m without a reflector. Its built-in Qualcomm octa-core processor ensures smooth operation. The CTS-A100 is perfect for everyday surveying tasks such as construction layout and topographic mapping, providing accurate and reliable data collection.
